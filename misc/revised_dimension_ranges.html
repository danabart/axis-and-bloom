Dimension What changed & why Key separator?
Sweetness Chocolate bumped up (8→11). Dark chocolate + nutty profiles have real perceived sweetness from development — caramel, toffee, cocoa. Giving it 8–11 lets it share the top sweetness band with Balanced but for a different reason (roast development vs. intrinsic caramel).
Fruity dropped (7→5–7) — acidity reads as brightness, not sweetness. Spicy & Earthy dropped to 4–6, it's the savory/dry end of the spectrum.
 Medium — overlaps intentionally between Choc & Balanced
Acidity No changes to the core three — already well calibrated. Floral stays at 10–12 (just below Fruity), which correctly reflects that floral coffees are bright but slightly gentler than washed Ethiopian naturals. ★ Best separator — clear gradient across all 5
Bitterness Balanced dropped to 4–6 (from 6–8) — the "sweet & smooth" drinker actively avoids bitterness. Fruity dropped to 2–4 (from 5–7) — fruity/floral drinkers are the most bitterness-averse of all. Floral also moved to 2–4 to match. Strong separator — Choc/Earthy vs. Floral/Fruity are now clearly opposite ends
Body No changes. Already the cleanest dimension. Spicy & Earthy stays heaviest (12–14), then Chocolate, then Balanced, then Floral/Fruity lightest. This correctly reflects French press vs. pour-over drinkers. ★ Best separator — no overlap between any pair
Texture Fruity moved to 1–3 (from 3–5) — cleanest mouthfeel of all archetypes, almost water-like. Chocolate moved to 4–7 — silky but with some structure from oils and roast. Floral stays at 2–4 (clean/silky). Spicy & Earthy stays at 9–11 (dry/structured). This dimension now has full separation. Improved — was flat before, now clearly differentiated
Savory / Depth Slight upward shift across the board for Chocolate (7→8–11) and Balanced (4→5–7) to give more breathing room. Fruity drops to 2–4 — these coffees are transparent, not complex. Floral stays at 3–5, slightly above Fruity because floral aromatics add perceived complexity. Strong separator — Earthy at 12–14 is an extreme anchor
Finish Length Biggest change: Fruity dropped from 7–9 to 3–5. Washed/fruity coffees have a bright snap finish that disappears quickly — that's a defining characteristic. Floral also moves to 3–5 (delicate, fades fast). Spicy & Earthy keeps the longest finish at 12–14 — the aftertaste is part of what people love about that profile. Improved — Fruity was overlapping with Balanced before, now clearly separated
